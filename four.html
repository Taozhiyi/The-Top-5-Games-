Fortnite

About the Game

Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. It is available in three distinct game mode versions that share the same general gameplay and game engine: Fortnite: Save the World, Fortnite Battle Royale, and Fortnite Creative.

Game Modes

  • Battle Royale: A 100-player PvP mode where players fight to be the last one standing.
  • Save the World: A cooperative survival game where players fight zombie-like creatures.
  • Creative: A sandbox mode where players can create their own islands and game modes.

Why Play Fortnite?

Fortnite is popular for its dynamic gameplay, frequent updates, and creative collaborations with popular franchises. It offers exciting in-game events, tournaments, and a thriving online community.
