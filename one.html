A Legendary History

EA Sports FC, formerly known as FIFA, has been the gold standard of soccer video games for decades. With cutting-edge graphics, realistic physics, and immersive gameplay, the franchise has evolved to create the ultimate football experience for fans worldwide.

The Heart of the Game - Database and Player Ratings

One of the key aspects of EA Sports FC’s success is its unparalleled database of players, teams, and leagues. With over 700 teams and 30+ leagues, the game features real-life players, stadiums, and kits. Player ratings are updated based on their real-world performance, reflecting their strengths and weaknesses in the game.

Game Modes – The Ultimate Football Experience

EA Sports FC offers a variety of gameplay modes to suit all types of players. From casual fans to competitive esports players, there's something for everyone:

  • Career Mode: Manage or play as your favorite team.
  • Pro Clubs: Join with friends and create your own club.
  • Volta Football: Enjoy street soccer with unique gameplay.
